We believe good care starts with relationships and communication. That’s why all of our digital tools are designed to strengthen the connection between women and their care providers. Our approach puts the woman at the center of her care, with total access to her health information, and tools and resources to help her manage her own health in collaboration with her care team. Our products support clinical care, patient engagement, and quality improvement. Every product in the Maternity Neighborhood comes with HIPAA-compliant web-based access from anywhere, secure messaging with clients and practice partners and file storage.
